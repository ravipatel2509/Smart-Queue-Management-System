About This Project

The Problem

In many public places like banks and hospitals, people have to stand in long queues for hours. This leads to crowding, frustration, and waste of time.

The Solution

The Smart Queue Management System is a simple web portal that allows users to book a token from their mobile or computer. They can see the live status of the queue and arrive only when their turn is near.

Key Advantages

  • Reduces physical crowding at the venue.
  • Saves time for customers.
  • More organized and efficient service.
  • Easy to use interface.
